• Recipe: Chocolate Cake

    • Ingredients
      • Dry Ingredients
        • Flour — 2 cups
          • Sifted
        • Sugar — 1 3/4 cups
          • Preferably granulated
        • Cocoa powder — 3/4 cup
          • Unsweetened
      • Wet Ingredients
        • Eggs — 2
          • Room temperature
        • Milk — 1 cup
          • Whole milk for best texture
        • Vegetable oil — 1/2 cup
          • Or melted butter
      • Extras
        • Vanilla extract — 2 tsp
        • Salt — 1 tsp
        • Baking powder — 1 tsp
    • Instructions

      • Step 1: Preheat oven

        • Preheat to 180°C / 350°F
          • Use the center rack

      • Step 2: Combine dry ingredients

        • Whisk flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, and salt.
          • Ensure even distribution

      • Step 3: Mix wet ingredients

        • Beat eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla.
          • Do not overmix

      • Step 4: Combine and bake

        • Fold wet into dry until just combined.
          • Pour into greased pan
          • Bake:
            • 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean
    • Topping / Frosting
      • Chocolate frosting
        • Cream butter and cocoa, slowly add powdered sugar and milk.
        • Spread when cake is completely cool.
